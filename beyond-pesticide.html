Beyond Pesticides



Beyond Pesticides is a group that works towards the betterment of health, biodiversity, and climate. Their main purpose is to improve the quality of life for the many inhabitants of this planet, human and not. Pesticides and chemicals are one of the main factors to the degredation of our Earth. These pesticides are not as regulated as they should be and proper legislation is not being pushed forward to ensure this.

By donated to Beyond Pesticides, you directly contribute to informing and advocating for safer practices of deadly chemicals.
